Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Introduction

Everyday Deals UK respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Amazon to eBay listing tool.

Data We Access

When you connect your eBay account, we access:

  • Your eBay seller account information
  • Inventory management permissions
  • Listing and fulfillment permissions

How We Use Your Data

We use your eBay account data only to:

  • Create and manage product listings on your behalf
  • Update inventory and pricing information
  • Process orders and fulfillment

Data Storage

Your eBay OAuth tokens are stored securely and encrypted. We do not store your eBay password.

Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with any third parties. Your information is used exclusively for the functionality of our service.

Your Rights

You can disconnect your eBay account at any time. You can also request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@everydaydeals.co.uk
